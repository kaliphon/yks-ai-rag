## 📚 What is yks-ai-rag?

yks-ai-rag is an application built to help students preparing for the YKS, the Turkish university entrance exam. It uses AI technology to solve problems and explain topics step-by-step. This software leverages Google Gemini API and FAISS, but you don't need to understand these to use the program.

The goal is to make study sessions easier by providing clear solutions and explanations. Whether you struggle with math problems or want to verify your answers, yks-ai-rag stands ready to support your learning.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Before you download and install, check if your device meets these requirements:

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 or newer, macOS 10.15 or newer, or Ubuntu 18.04 and later.
- **Processor:** 1.8 GHz dual-core or better.
- **Memory:** 4 GB of RAM minimum; 8 GB recommended.
- **Storage:** At least 500 MB free space.
- **Internet:** Required for AI features to work, as it connects to Google Gemini API.
- **Python Version:** Python 3.8 or higher installed (only if running from source; pre-built releases include everything).

These specs ensure the app runs smoothly and efficiently.

## ⚙️ Using yks-ai-rag

Once running, you can start by entering a question or topic related to your YKS studies.
